## Interpretation

The sample supports a new candidate-generation method: a CIB-detected account may distribute risk evidence across several posts and replies rather than exposing every signal in one post.

Repo-safe style families observed in the controlled sample:

- profile-level free community / synchronized-operation / stock-question framing;
- past-performance and stock-picking proof;
- strong market-direction and broad bullish framing;
- individualized operation language such as switching, buying, adding, holding, or continuing positions;
- visible follower/social-proof style engagement counts in the account feed.

## Boundary

`account_multi_post_style_cluster` is not a standalone scam label for every post under the account. It is a candidate-discovery and repeat-source context method. Any selected candidate still needs its own full-thread capture, redacted manual entry, strict validation, and second review before entering the aggregate dataset.
