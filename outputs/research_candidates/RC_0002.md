# RC_0002: Guarantee Yield Signal Survived First Reviewed Expansion

## Title

Guarantee or certainty claims as a low-burden high-yield review signal

## Observation

After Batch 0004 added 10 human-reviewed structured metadata records, `保證收益` increased coverage and improved SVS.

`成果展示` moved in the opposite direction: once reviewed across more candidates, it became more uncertain and burden-sensitive.

## Evidence

- Source batch: `batch-0004-low-coverage-high-svs`
- Candidate records written: 10
- Candidate records total after run: 14
- `保證收益` SVS: `0.0068438 -> 0.015351855`
- `保證收益` reviewed count: `1 -> 6`
- `保證收益` yield rate after run: `1.0`
- `成果展示` SVS: `0.0068438 -> 0.00358105`
- `成果展示` reviewed count: `1 -> 9`
- Batch 0004 SVS: `0.005125224`
- Discrepancy cases after run: `0`

## Hypothesis

`保證收益` is a stronger sparse discovery signal than broad `成果展示` because certainty or guarantee structure reduces reviewer ambiguity while preserving high research yield.

`成果展示` should be treated as a mixed signal unless paired with contact movement, funnel structure, or explicit value certainty.

## Next Experiment

Create a capped contrast batch:

```text
A: 保證收益 + funnel/contact structure
B: 成果展示 without explicit 保證收益
```

Measure whether arm A keeps higher SVS and lower second-review pressure, and whether arm B needs a new burden-aware subfeature before it should guide exploration.
