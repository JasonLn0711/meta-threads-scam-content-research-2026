## Hypothesis

`成果展示 AND needs_thread AND NOT 保證收益` is not a direct high-yield signal.
It is a reviewer-burden feature that can help route cases before full review.

Proposed feature:

```text
feature_id: context_required_result_display
activation_rule: 成果展示 AND needs_thread AND NOT 保證收益
```
