# Summary Usefulness Rubric

Use this rubric to score repo-safe candidate summaries in assisted review.

Do not quote raw Threads content, raw OCR, handles, URLs, screenshots, controlled-store locators, or stakeholder case IDs.

## Rating Scale

| Rating | Meaning | Decision Use |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Summary is misleading, omits decisive context, or increases review burden | Reject summary design |
| 2 | Summary is partially useful but requires substantial correction or full reread | Revise summary design |
| 3 | Summary helps orientation but does not materially reduce review work | Keep shadow-only |
| 4 | Summary reduces reading burden and preserves uncertainty | Continue evaluation |
| 5 | Summary substantially reduces burden without hiding hard-negative or missing-evidence issues | Candidate for expansion |
